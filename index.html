Projects

Investment Approvals Application

Innovation Lead – RSK Group Ltd

In collaboration with digital solutions colleagues, Group IT, and Finance, I spearheaded the design, development, testing, and implementation of a comprehensive group-wide approval process using Power Platform, SharePoint, and O365. This initiative replaced a manual system, introducing a bespoke automated solution that supported operational efficiency and transparency while concurrently reducing administration costs and errors.

The system architecture employed a multi-page PowerApps Canvas App integrated with related SharePoint Lists for streamlined data storage and look-ups. The approval pathway was dynamically configured, responding to a range of inputs, and executed seamlessly through PowerAutomate. To ensure user-friendly interactions, a guided form was hosted in both SharePoint and Teams, and accessible through embedded email responses. Users navigated the system with ease, guided by concise prompts and reminders tailored to the specific context of their interactions.

This project exemplifies my commitment to leveraging digital tools to create tailored solutions that not only meet organizational needs but also enhance user experience and operational effectiveness.

Award-Winning Tracking and Support Hub

Innovation Lead – RSK Group Ltd

In response to identifying substantial lost effort and sales within our major bids’ functions, I collaborated with colleagues to develop a comprehensive tracking and support hub. This innovative solution, seamlessly integrated with group ERP systems, delivered timely and relevant information to key stakeholders, resulting in a notable increase in the success rate of major bids.

The core of the system was a dynamic PowerApps Canvas App intricately linked with related SharePoint Lists. To achieve integration with the existing ERP system, a combination of PowerShell, Excel (utilising ODBC), and PowerAutomate was employed. This integration facilitated the flow of data, ensuring that stakeholders had access to the most up-to-date information.

The reporting functionality was robustly enabled through a PowerBI Dashboard, empowering leaders to dynamically interrogate data and derive valuable insights. This project exemplifies my ability to identify operational inefficiencies, collaborate on innovative solutions, and implement systems that drive tangible improvements in key performance metrics.

Digital Environmental Impact Assessment

Environmental Planner & Innovation Lead – RSK Group Ltd

I worked on a concept for Digital Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) over a period of approximately 4 years. This initially entailed talking to clients and colleagues about the purpose, value and audience for EIA and the conceptual benefits and viability of digitisation.

Presenting to a major strategic land client I was met with the response "the only people who read an EIA are you and the lawyers, Mark". Whilst a slightly glib remark it reinforced the complex nature of this service and the need to be clear about the value you endeavour to generate. In response I conducted a review of case law summarising key judgements and their influence on practice.

Later I convened a design thinking session, bringing together subject matter experts from across the group for a day long session. We identified and idealised customers, generated value propositions, brainstormed solutions, appraised viability and identified preferred options to progress. The ability to distill clear direction in complexity and bring people with you was key to this project.

Multivariant Analysis & Research

Sustainability Advisor – Matilda’s Planet

In the capacity of advising investors on renewable technologies, I undertook a comprehensive research initiative involving both primary and secondary research methods. Focusing on solar thermal technologies, I took a unique approach by reverse-engineering the SAP calculator and conducting a multivariant analysis to discern the key drivers of returns under the Renewable Heat Incentive (RHI).

This meticulous analysis was instrumental in presenting a nuanced understanding to the board. By adopting my recommendations, the board demonstrated a strategic alignment with the identified key drivers, ultimately enhancing the effectiveness of their commercial evaluation process.

The project's significance lay in the recognition that, at the time, effective commercial evaluation of solar thermal technology necessitated a nuanced appreciation of the distinction between a technologically sound solution and one poised to yield returns under the RHI. This project showcases my ability to apply both technical and strategic insights to provide actionable recommendations in complex decision-making scenarios.

Data and Process Mapping

Innovation Lead – RSK Group Ltd

Collaborating closely with key stakeholders, including the Group Sustainability Lead, Reporting Lead, Facilities Management, Finance, and IT, I spearheaded the creation of a comprehensive data and process map for carbon emissions calculations. This initiative covered a thorough examination of data sources, processes, and calculations specifically focused on Scope 1 & 2 emissions.

The project aimed to enhance transparency and efficiency in carbon emissions reporting. Through meticulous mapping, we streamlined the data flow and calculation processes, ensuring accuracy and consistency in reporting. Notably, I worked closely with the team to create a robust framework that aligned with industry standards and organizational goals.

While Scope 3 emissions were initially out of scope for mapping, I took the initiative to evaluate various technologies for data gathering, especially from sources such as PDFs. This proactive approach demonstrated my commitment to continuous improvement and staying abreast of emerging technologies to enhance the comprehensiveness of our emissions data.

This project showcases my ability to collaborate across diverse functional areas, aligning technical processes with sustainability goals, and proactively exploring opportunities for process optimization and technological advancements.

Data Analytics and Visualisation

Open Data Institute

As part of my strategic data course, I conducted an in-depth appraisal of publicly available data from the London Fire Brigade. This project showcased my proficiency in the end-to-end data analysis process, from raw data handling to the creation of impactful visualisations, all while maintaining a keen awareness of ethical considerations.
